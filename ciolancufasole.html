Ingredients

  • 1 pig smoked knuckle, 4-600g
  • 400 grams white beans
  • 2 onions
  • 1 carrots
  • 3 garlic cloves optional
  • 1 red pepper optional
  • 100 ml passata
  • 100 ml oil
  • 4 bay leaves
  • black peppercorns
  • thyme
  • paprika
  • pepper
  • salt
  • fresh parsley optional

Instructions

  1. Put the white beans to soak in water for 10 to 15 hours. Overnight is good.
  2. Drain and wash the beans with some more water.
  3. Put the beans in a large pan and cover with water.
  4. Give it a boil.
  5. Drain and throw the water. By doing so you ensure that the beans are a lot easier to digest.
  6. Wash them again with warm water.
  7. Put the beans back in the pan and add the ham in too.
  8. Cover it with 3-4 litters of boiling water(from the kettle is good).
  9. Add the carrots, parsley and bay leaf.
  10. Simmer the beans over low heat until it is cooked well. It can take up to 2 hours in a normal pan,
  11. Add the tomato paste, salt and thyme to taste.

  15. Turn off the hob and leave it to sit for 10 more minutes or so with the lid on.
